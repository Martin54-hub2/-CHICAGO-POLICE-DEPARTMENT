Department Offices

Organizational structure of the Chicago Police Department

Office of the Superintendent

Superintendent B. Stafford

The Department is led by the Superintendent of Police. In addition to overall Department management, the Office of the Superintendent is responsible for planning and implementing the Community Policing Strategy, facilitating law enforcement services, planning police coverage at public gatherings, and providing media liaison.

Bureaus & Divisions

The CPD is organized into several major bureaus

🕵 Bureau of Detectives

Investigates major crimes including homicide, robbery, burglary, and financial crimes. Works closely with community members and other agencies to solve cases and bring offenders to justice.

Divisions: Homicide, Robbery, Burglary, Financial Crimes, Special Victims

🚶 Bureau of Patrol

Responsible for field operations including the protection of life and property, apprehension of criminals, and enforcement of traffic laws and ordinances across all 22 police districts.

Covers: Districts 1-22, Traffic Division, Special Events

⚖ Bureau of Internal Affairs

Investigates allegations of misconduct by department members. Ensures accountability, professional standards, and maintains public trust in the department.

Units: Professional Standards, Investigations, Civilian Complaints

🎯 Bureau of Counter-Terrorism

Coordinates counter-terrorism operations, intelligence gathering, and works with federal agencies to prevent and respond to threats against the city.

Units: Intelligence, Bomb & Arson, SWAT, HBT

📚 Education & Training

Provides comprehensive instruction for new recruits at the Police Academy and continuing education for all active officers. Develops curriculum and training standards.

Programs: Academy, Field Training, In-Service, Firearms Qualification

🤝 Community Policing

Implements the Community Policing Strategy, coordinates beat meetings, manages the community engagement calendar, and strengthens police-community relationships.

Programs: CAPS, Beat Meetings, Youth Programs, Community Events
