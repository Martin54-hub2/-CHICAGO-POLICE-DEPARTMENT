Service Catalog

Resources and services available to the public and department members

The Chicago Police Department offers several services for its citizens including purchasing crime and traffic reports, registering information, reporting tips, requesting data, and searching our databases.

Purchase

Reports & Records

Purchase copies of crime reports, traffic crash reports, and other official documents from the Chicago Police Department.

  • Crime / Incident Reports
  • Traffic Crash Reports
  • Accident Records
  • Case File Copies
Register

Registration Services

Register your property, vehicles, or security cameras with the Chicago Police Department for enhanced protection.

  • Bicycle Registration
  • Security Camera Registration
  • Business Alarm Registration
  • Vehicle Registration Records
Report

Report & Submit Tips

Report crimes, suspicious activity, or submit anonymous tips to help the CPD keep our communities safe.

  • File a Police Report
  • Anonymous Tip Line
  • Drug Tip Hotline
  • Report Suspicious Activity
Request

Data & Information Requests

Request data, statistics, or information from the Chicago Police Department for research or personal use.

  • FOIA Requests
  • Crime Statistics
  • District Data
  • Special Event Information
Search

Search Databases

Search the CPD's public databases for crime data, registries, and other publicly available information.

  • CLEARmap Crime Data
  • Sex Offender Registry
  • Arrest Records
  • Most Wanted Database
Emergency Services

Emergency Contact

In case of emergency, always call 911 first. For non-emergency situations, contact your local district station.

Emergency: 911
Non-Emergency: (312) 555-1234
Anonymous Tips: (312) 555-TIPS
